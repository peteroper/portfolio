About Resume Dark Mode

Elevating User Experiences : A Senior UX Designers role at Asurion

Morning

9am ~ 10am

Focus & Itemize

I often use this time to focus on refining large ongoing tasks and itemizing what needs to be conveyed in the morning meetings.

10am ~ 11am

Daily Content team sync

I’m currently the lead designer for the content team while working on a multi sprint release for a learning content feature.

Daily Design team standup

The standup is an open floor to share progress and get feedback, however an underlying goal is to build relationships amongst our hybrid/remote team.

11am

Review & Sync

  • Depending on the day a weekly catch-up with specific team members.
  • some focused time to review my own or other tasks' progress.
  • check I’m up to date with teams' discussions and make sure requests and deliverables are being met for the day.
  • Drop a topic in the design discussion thread to get the team chatting or sync 1on1 to map out upcoming tasks or initiatives.

Lunch

My fast ends so I enjoy a packed salad and aim to get outside and potter around my garden. Prepping some food for the evening's meal helps me relax and reset my thoughts.

Afternoon

1pm ~ 5pm

Work Block & Syncs

Afternoons have a more open schedule which allows me to time block larger work periods and sync with project team members.

Weekly Meetings

  1. App & Content backlog refinement meetings
  2. client review
  3. Biz and KPI review
  4. Various UX Research meetings - UT schedule, tasks, insights

Monthly Meetings

  1. Front End team feature walkthrough
  2. User story walkthroughs

3pm

Take a Break

Depending on my workload I try to take a few breaks during the afternoon which help me focus my thoughts.

My kids and wife are returning home so it’s a good chance to switch off for 15 minutes and socialize and engage in casual talk. Doing a few chores around the house gives my day balance and I feel I’m making progress on both fronts.

4:30pm

Reflect & Plan

I like to get some more sun in before finishing off my day. I either sit on the balcony or take my dog for a short walk. I use this time to reflect on the day and outline tomorrow’s objectives in Notion.

5pm ~ 6pm

Refine & Discuss

  • Like my first morning block I switch to refining the day's work and provide any missing deliverables for the day.
  • Broader discussions with PM’s and Design leads often happen which help us align on a shared goal.
