Football at your finger tips

Pulse Football is a one stop indepth app for football fans who need more than just the news.

Many football fans obsess over the tiniest piece of information. However finding this information involves traversing numerous platforms and media types to extract the truth.

With the onset of AI various tasks and aggregations can be performed to optimise and present information simply and in one location. Allowing the pulse football user to tailor what information they require.
