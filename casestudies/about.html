For the last 3 years I have been furthering my skills as a UX Designer at Asurion Japan, a US multinational smartphone insurance and tech-support provider.

As a senior designer within Asurion Japan's UX team, I have been part of a group of 10~15 designers and researchers who operate within the product development department.

Over the past two years, I have worked on Asurion’s flagship product, an au support application for KDDI. Previous responsibilities at Asurion saw me lead the design tasks for CMS, Admin tools and Chat Systems. In addition to supporting the localization of the companies photo storage app.

Personality

I have a curious and adaptive mindset which means I spend a lot of my time in and out of work exploring and iterating solutions to problems. I often anticipate needs and respond with early outlines and step by step plans to overcome the challenge.

Whilst being creative minded I am also very methodical and organised. I value hard work and believe increasing performance in the short term leads to better outcomes. I also value humor, good relationships and paying an interest to the people in my life. Empathy and communication are another way i engage creativity.

Values

  • Composition & Layout

    Composition grounds a lot of my work and thinking on design. With its connection to user perception, paying attention to detailing can be the difference between good and excellent.

  • Human Psychology

    Human behaviour and the reasoning behind peoples actions intrigues me. With a combination of intuition and study of psychology I outline solutions with a user centric approach.

  • Iteration & Planning

    I’m one of life’s list makers and a bit of an over thinker. With self awareness I keep it in check and harness it to get a head of the curve work wise and anticipate future challenges.

  • Team-work & Collaboration

    I’m a team player and do my best to support and motivate the people around me. I also respect each team members unique talent and perspective which they can share to increase output and productivity.

Interests

Many of my hobbies are outlets for creative problem solving and have parallels with product development. I enjoy long term projects like gardening and interior design which focus on planning, composition and adapting to changing circumstances. Recently I have been enjoying cooking and craft beers and would like to try to make my own ales!
