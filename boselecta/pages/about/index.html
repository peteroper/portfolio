About me

Leading design
with clarity and execution,
bringing structure to complexity.

I’m a design generalist who has evolved over a 20+ year career, continuously expanding my skill set. My love for design comes from the journey of building tangible solutions & refining elements into something meaningful and impactful.

Over the last five years, working within a large Japanese–US product development team, I’ve honed my process, execution, and delivery skills—becoming someone who supports a wide range of roles, from PMs and BAs to TAs, QAs, and developers. I’m known for getting the work done and making complex challenges appear effortless.

"I value the full end-to-end product lifecycle, contributing not just to initial delivery but to long-term, continuous improvement. That takes more than skill—it takes stamina, adaptability, and ongoing commitment."

In my current role I have experienced a broad range of projects. In the last year I have worked on the launch of a new Asurion Home Protection service which involved, localisation, responsive UI design, marketing and system-as-a-service thinking. I’ve also been a lead designer on a long-term initiative designing a popular consumer app for au, delivered in agile sprints. In parallel, I’ve supported various internal tools, including chat platforms and admin systems. These experiences have deepened my versatility as a designer, allowing me to adapt seamlessly across product types, teams, and design challenges.

At the core of my work is a deep focus on people—both the users I design for and the colleagues I collaborate with. I pay close attention to patterns and take an empathetic, considered approach to understanding feelings, uncovering needs, and creating solutions that make a meaningful difference.

Alittle about me...

I take a calm, observant approach to life and design. I’ve lived in Japan for over 20 years, and find a lot of joy in walking, gardening, and shaping my surroundings with care. For me, good design feels like a well-tended space—it grows, adapts, and reveals meaning over time.
