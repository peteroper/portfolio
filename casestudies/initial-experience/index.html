Project

I work as a Senior UX Designer for the Tsukasapo project. One of my tasks is to lead upstream concept design creation by synthesising research insights and converting into prototypes for Road Map and Backlog items.

Product

The Tsukasapo app is a paid premium tech support & solutions service provided as part of a broader device protection service for au (KDDI).

Context

Senior and Low literacy users account for x% of the .

Problem

The Tsukasapo service has over 4million paid users however less than 50% of users have progressed beyond the apps provisioning flow and a further drop in users is seen after day 1 viewing is completed.

Pain Points

  1. The apps value proposition is not clear to users.
  2. Provisioning has many steps which require high level of user comprehension.
  3. App UI is text rich and requires high level of user comprehension.
