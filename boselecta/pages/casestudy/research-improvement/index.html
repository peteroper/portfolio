Structure

I made a conscious decision to map the current and ideal flows. I sometimes skip ahead due to time restrictions but having come off the back of a different task breaking down due to unrefined requirements, I thought it best to outline early and get support for my idea at a basic level.

I’m confident in my ability to visualise internally but by focussing on this layer of detail I was satisfied that I had captured a refined and simplified flow which aided different user needs and pain points.
