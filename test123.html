Showcase

Pulse Football Adventure Sports Kokonotori Navi

Pulse Football is a one stop indepth app for fans who need more than just the news.

Many football fans obsess over the tiniest piece of information. However finding this information involves searching across numerous websites and media platforms to get a comprehensive understanding of whats happening on a daily basis.

Product Overview

  1. All in one application: news, analysis & community.
  2. Apply IA to simplify information gathering.
  3. Refine information based on user interests.
  4. Create Seemless UX with interaction and transitions.

Football at your finger tips

Over the last 7-8 years I have noticed football related products becoming more sophisticated. Notable applications would include Fantasy Premier League, Transfermarkt and One Football.

Although One Football is pushing boundaries, football news still feels trapped in an outdated media format, where information is spread across multiple articles.

Thanks to the advent of AI, it is now possible to aggregate and combine football information to cater to users' specific needs. This includes providing comprehensive analysis, data insights, and an all-in-one football experience.

Objectives
