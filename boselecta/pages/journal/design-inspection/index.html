Syncing a Design inspection alongside UAT

A hands-on review of a release helps bridge the gap between design intent and final execution, ensuring smooth developer collaboration and a refined user experience.

What needed Fixing

I understand the Front End coding phase well. Initially, you implement the layout to match the Figma designs, then refine and align it more closely after taking a step back. In a recent product release, I noticed some details were missed, which can happen. During UAT, these nuances often get overlooked. To ensure the product was ready for launch, I led a design inspection process, working closely with the development team to align the final implementation with the original design intent.

"Design doesn't stop at handoff; it’s essential to see it through to implementation, ensuring the vision is realized in the final product."
