Defining the problem and solution and measuring the success

Problem

The Tsukasapo service has over 4million paid subsribers however less than 50% of customers have progressed beyond the tsukasapo apps provisioning flow and a further drop in active users is seen after day 1 viewing is completed.

Pain Points

  1. Value Proposition

    The apps value proposition is not clear to users.

  2. Long Provisioning

    Provisioning has many steps which require high level of user comprehension.

  3. User Comprehension

    App UI is text rich and requires high level of user comprehension.
